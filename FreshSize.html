Information about FreshSize

FreshSize is an application that will tell you your pant size across multiple brands and lines. Are you tired of taking three different sizes of jeans into the dressing room? FreshSize will tell you with accuracy the size you are for any brand that you are looking for. Shop online with 100% confidence knowing that you’ll get the perfect fit every time. Our revolutionary application synchronizes your size across brand names you’ve never even shopped in before. Click on this Brands link to see all the different brands that you could try out without having to try on any pants!

Our company was founded on November 2016. We are based in Indianapolis, Indiana with the goal of helping others in the effort of saving them time and money in the search of new pants.
